Our Services

Curative Services: Outpatient and inpatient care, specialist consultations, surgical procedures.
Public Health Services: Immunization programs, health education campaigns, disease surveillance.
Policy, Informatics, Monitoring & Evaluation: Health data analysis, reporting systems, health policy development initiatives.
Finance: Budgeting processes, funding for health programs, financial accountability measures.
Human Resource Management: Workforce planning, training programs, professional development opportunities.
Administration and Logistics: Facility management, supply chain management for medical supplies, procurement processes.
General Inspectorate of Health Services: Inspections of healthcare facilities, monitoring service standards, enforcing regulations.
Community Engagement: Health fairs, community workshops, partnerships with local organizations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What services does the MoHCC offer?
A: The MoHCC provides curative, preventive, public health, and administrative health services across Zimbabwe.

Q: How can I access health services?
A: You can visit any public hospital, clinic, or outreach center, or contact your local provincial health office.

Q: What should I do in case of a medical emergency?
A: Call the national emergency number or go to the nearest hospital immediately.

Q: Where can I find health resources and information?
A: Visit the MoHCC website or contact the nearest provincial health office.

If you have any complaints regarding our services, please contact us directly at the help desk number or email.

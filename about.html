About Us

The Harare Provincial Metropolitan Department (PMD) is a division of the Ministry of Health and Child Care, dedicated to improving public health outcomes in Harare Province.

  • Vision: A healthy and productive Harare Province.
  • Mission: To provide accessible, equitable, and quality health services to all residents.
  • Core Values: Integrity, Professionalism, Compassion, Innovation.
